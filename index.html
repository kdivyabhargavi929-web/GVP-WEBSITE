About GVPCE

Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering is an autonomous institution approved by AICTE and affiliated to JNTU. Established with a vision to provide quality technical education, the college is known for academic excellence and industry collaborations.

Mission & Vision

  • Provide world‑class engineering education.
  • Promote innovation, research, and entrepreneurship.
  • Develop socially responsible professionals.

Accreditations

  • NAAC Accredited with Grade A.
  • NBA Accredited for multiple programs.
  • Approved by AICTE, New Delhi.
